Research Areas

We pursue interdisciplinary research spanning core AI, robot perception, control, and real-world deployments in healthcare, logistics, and manufacturing.

Autonomous Systems

Planning, control, multi-agent coordination, and safety for mobile robots and autonomous vehicles.

Perception & ML

Sensor fusion, trajectory prediction, computer vision, and probabilistic machine learning for robust perception.

Human-Robot Interaction

User-centered interaction, assistive robotics, intent recognition, and explainability for trust and collaboration.
